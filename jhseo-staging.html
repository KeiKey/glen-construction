Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed and insured for driveway work in New Jersey?

Yes. At Glen Construction, we are fully registered and insured to complete driveway services across New Jersey. Our team follows all local codes and safety standards on every project, whether we are handling a residential driveway or larger commercial paving work. As experienced driveway contractors, we take pride in protecting your property while delivering durable results on all surfaces (including pavers, stone, concrete, gravel, and asphalt surfaces).

Do I need permits for a new driveway installation?

In many cases, yes. Permits are often required for a new driveway, especially if grading, drainage, or access points are being changed. Requirements vary by municipality. We guide you through the process and handle the necessary steps, so your paving project stays compliant and moves forward without delays.

How long does driveway paving take?

Most driveway paving projects can be completed within 2 to 3 days (including asphalt driveway paving), depending on the size, layout, and site conditions. Larger or more complex projects, including commercial paving or parking lot installations, can take up to a week or longer. We schedule quickly and work weekends when needed to keep your project on track.

How much does a new driveway cost?

Pricing depends on size, site preparation, materials, and design. We start with an in-person visit to evaluate your space, take measurements, and review layout options. This helps us create a plan that supports both function and curb appeal. Once finalized, you can expect a clear quote within 1 to 2 days with no surprises.

Do you provide contracts for driveway projects?

Yes. Every project includes a detailed written agreement outlining the scope of work, materials, timeline, and payment structure.

Who manages the project on site?

Our team at Glen Construction manages every job. As trusted paving contractors, we stay hands-on throughout the entire process, whether we are installing an asphalt driveway or completing a full parking lot. You will always know who to contact and what to expect at every stage.

Can I still use my property during installation?

In most cases, yes. For residential driveway projects, you can remain in your home while work is completed, though you may need to enter your home from other areas than the driveway. For commercial projects, we plan carefully to maintain safe access for customers and operations whenever possible.

How do I maintain my driveway after installation?

Routine maintenance helps extend the life of your driveway. We recommend seal coating every few years to protect against weather, water, and wear. Our professional seal coating services help preserve the surface and keep it looking clean and well-maintained.

Do you install driveways for businesses as well?

Yes. We work with both homeowners and businesses, offering everything from residential driveway installations to large-scale commercial paving projects, including parking lot construction and resurfacing. Our team builds surfaces designed to handle daily traffic while improving overall appearance.

Will a new driveway improve the look of my property?

Yes. A professionally installed driveway instantly upgrades curb appeal and creates a clean, finished look for your home or business. If you are replacing an old surface or starting fresh, the right design and materials can make a noticeable difference.
